### Tic-Tac-Toe

Tic-tac-toe is a game for two players, X and O, who takes turns marking the spaces in a 3√ó3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.

In this game you and your friend will chose a position from the board and you will try to win before the other player do.
